About me

Hey!

My name is Simo Aleksandrov and I'm a 16 year old kiddo from Ruse, Bulgaria.
I've been interested in programming since when I was in the 4th grade, but only recently got more into it!
I study at the High School of Mathematics "Baba Tonka" and am in the 10th grade.
I've got a bunch of hobbies and interests, some of which include programming, playing videogames, watching anime, listening to music and more!
I love music genres such as post-rock, psytrance and more!
You can check out my last.fm in the links tab.
Some of my favorite anime shows are Made in Abyss, Madoka Magica, Steins;Gate and Little Witch Academia!
I usually play either osu! or CPMA in my free time.
I mainly code in C# and JavaScript, but would also like to work with C++.
I'm a big fan of frameworks. Some that I've used and/or like are Electron, Materialize, Vue.js and React Native.
